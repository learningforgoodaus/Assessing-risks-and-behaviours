Digital Safety Risks

Tech & Monitoring

Safety is compromised through technology. This includes a barrage of calls or texts requiring an immediate response to "prove" location, the use of tracking apps or GPS on phones and cars to monitor movements, and communication sabotage where perpetrators call the workplace repeatedly to disrupt work and maintain control.

Observable Red Flags

Behavioural Signs

Watch for sudden changes indicating a crisis. This includes acute panic or hypervigilance after a phone call, unexplained injuries often hidden by concealing clothing (like scarves in heat), and attendance sabotage where the perpetrator steals car keys, denies childcare, or prevents the employee from leaving home.
